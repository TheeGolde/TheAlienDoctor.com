Community Guidelines of The Alien Empire

Welcome to The Alien Empire!

Below you can find the rules and guidelines for The Alien Empire community! These rules extend to everywhere The Alien Empire is, e.g. Discord server, YouTube comments, Stream chat etc.

Essentially, its just one place for me to put all the rules for all the different places that my community is!

Rules

Spamming

Please refrain from spamming messages or sending messages that may be seen as spam. This could include long paragraphs, lots of back-to-back messages, excessive emoji/stickers or caps and "ASCII art". Our aim is to be able to have a clear and coherent conversation that flows well, if your not contrbuting to that then whats the point in messaging? Please do not spam ping, and do not spam or send messages purely for levels

Please stay PG-13 and SFW

Due to Alien's content being aimed at those 13 or over, and being enjoyed by a wide range of ages we ask that all content is family friendly.

Discriminatory Language

The Alien Empire community aims to be open and friendly to all. Because of this, under no circumstances will derogatory behaviour be tolerated. This could include use of sexist, homophobic and ableist slurs or statements and jokes relating to those subjects, as well as thing ssuch as age, gender, disability etc. are also strictly not tolerated. We are not asking you to have the same political opinion as us, just be respectful.

Conflict

Please avoid talking about subjects that are often dividing, e.g. religion and politcs. Please also avoid bringing conflict to our community. If any conflict has starting in The Alien Empire community, please let the moderation team know via a DM to ModMail.

Doxxing and Harassment

Phising, sharing others personal data, threats to someones life/property or encouraging self hard are strcitly forbidden. Anything seen as doxxing is taken very seriously, and in some circumstances may get reported elsewhere. Harassment towards other members is also not allowed. "Its nice to be nice"

Contacting mods, pings and DMs

Please avoid DMing or contacting moderators, and Alien. Instead please DM ModMail on Discord. Pinging people for technical help and randomly pinging to try and get a conversation going is not allowed.
